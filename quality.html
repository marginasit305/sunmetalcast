Quality Assurance

The casting produce is as per ISO quality standards and quality tests are perform at various stages of process such as sand testing, mould compressive strength, scratch hardness of mould, online carbon equivalent meter, wet analysis, tensile strength, hardness etc. We give quality report & certificate with delivery of product which is a real meaning of quality control process.

Quality Control

  • Sand Testing Analysis

    • AFS Analysis
    • Permiability Testing
    • Compressive Strength

  • Corrosion Test

    • Corrosion test according to ASTM A262 Practice A, B, C, E & F
    • Pitting Corrosion Test as per ASTM G 48 Method A, E
    • Creative Corrosion as per ASTM G48 Method B

  • Non Destructive Testing

    • Dye Penetrate Testing
    • Magnetic Particle Testing: Wet Florasant Method & Dry Method
    • Radiography Testing: lr 192
    • Ultrasonic Testing

  • Mechanical Testing

    • UTM capacity of 200 KN
    • Electronic Extensometer for 0.2% proof stress.

  • Impact Testing

    • Capacity: 200 J max.
    • Testing Temp: From 20°C to -196°C

  • Micro Structure Examination

    • Ferrite Testing as per ASTM E 562 etc.
    • Micro Structure Examination as per ASTM A 923 etc.

  • Hardness Testing

    • Digital Rockwell Hardness Tester.
    • Policy Hardness Tester.

  • Spectrometer

    • Fe and Ni base Spectrometer having 27 elements with facility of N analysis.

  • Chemical Analysis

    • All type of Ferrous Material Analysis by Wet Method
